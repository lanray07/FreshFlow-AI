# FreshFlow AI Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 5, 2026

FreshFlow AI is a food organization and meal suggestion app for pantry, fridge, grocery, recipe, and food-waste planning.

## Data Collection

FreshFlow AI does not collect personal data from the app in its current release configuration.

Inventory items, household preferences, recipe prompts, voice command drafts, scan results, shopping lists, and waste reports are designed to remain on the user's device unless a future backend service is explicitly enabled and disclosed.

## Camera, Microphone, Speech, And Notifications

FreshFlow AI may request access to the camera, microphone, speech recognition, and notifications only for user-initiated app features such as scanning food, scanning receipts, entering voice commands, and receiving expiry reminders.

These permissions are optional. The app does not record in the background, does not use always-listening behavior, and provides typed alternatives for voice-enabled flows.

## Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. FreshFlow AI does not collect or store payment card information.

## Food Safety

FreshFlow AI is a food organization and meal suggestion tool. It does not provide medical nutrition advice, diagnose health conditions, certify food safety, guarantee food freshness, or replace official food safety guidance.

## Changes

If FreshFlow AI enables a backend service, remote AI processing, account sync, or household sharing that collects data, this privacy policy and the App Store privacy details must be updated before release.
